WELCOME TO JAYA YOGA!

Jaya Yoga Toronto provides students with an authentic experience that incorporates traditional yoga practices in our classes. The Jaya Yoga Tribe consists of a community of teachers and students who are dedictated to experiencing a life of well-being and are conscientious of the energy we share in the world. We celebrate bodies of all shapes and sizes, we welcome students of all ages and walks of life, and we are a safe haven for our LGBTQ community.

PRIVACY

We are committed to connection, inspiration, and learning what is possible in life through the practice of yoga, breathwork and meditation. We believe that through practice and inclusion, we can rise together as a global community and change the world!

LEGAL

We're a little out of the way, but take a drive down Garrett Bay Road to JOB today! Indulge in our locally roasted free-trade and home-made pastries. You'll feel right at home at JOB!

SPECIFIC INFORMATION

Our boutique yoga studio offers an elevated experience! We are not just a yoga studio, we are a yoga family. We offer a variety of workshops, specialty class series and have a robust meditation program.We are confident that you will find the yoga class, workshop or teacher training program you are searching for!
